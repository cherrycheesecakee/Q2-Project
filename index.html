Here, you'll find 4 different tourist locations you can visit on your next trip to the philippines! These places are located amongst the 7,641 islands of the Philippines. So, what are you waiting for? Let's go and discuss the 4 tourist locations in this webpage!

Calle Crisologo, Vigan

It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Ilocos. It has calesas(horse-drawn carriages) and delicious food.

Kawasan Falls, Cebu

A place with cool cascading waters, and is perfect for swimming. Located in Cebu, Kawanasan Falls, is a multi-layered waterfall, known for its turquoise waters.

Mayon Volcano, Albay

An active volcano located in the province of Albay. It's one of the most beautiful places to visit in the Philippines. It's name comes from the word magayon, which means beautiful.

Intramuros and Fort Santiago, Manila

A citadel which served as a headquarters for armies and foreign powers. The site where our natinal hero, Dr. Jose Rizal, was imprisoned.
